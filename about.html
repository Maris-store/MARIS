About MARIS

Born in Pakistan. Built for the world.

MARIS is a luxury footwear label for people who want modern sneakers with restraint, confidence, and craft.

Brand Story

The discipline of less.

MARIS began with a simple rejection of clutter. The brand studies proportion, shadow, leather textures, and street movement to create footwear that looks quiet from a distance and exceptional up close.

Our design language blends deep black, luxury navy, and warm beige into a timeless identity for Pakistan’s next generation of sneaker and fashion consumers.

Mission

Elevate daily movement

Create premium footwear that gives Pakistani consumers an international luxury experience.

Vision

A new regional standard

Build a fashion-forward footwear house recognized for taste, material discipline, and modern craft.

Why MARIS

Less logo. More presence.

A brand for young professionals, sneaker lovers, and streetwear enthusiasts who prefer refined details.

Craftsmanship

Designed through restraint.

Each silhouette is reviewed for balance: toe shape, panel depth, lace geometry, heel height, outsole weight, and comfort.

Concept

A pared-back brief focused on black, navy, beige, and premium versatility.

Material Study

Texture, durability, and comfort are tested before form is finalized.

Prototype

Panel lines are reduced, proportions are sharpened, and fit is refined.

Release

Limited drops keep the range focused and intentional.

Brand Philosophy

Luxury is the confidence to remove.

MARIS uses silence as a design tool: fewer colors, fewer marks, better shapes, better surfaces, and a calmer buying experience through direct WhatsApp ordering.
