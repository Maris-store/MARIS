Everything you need to know about MARIS sizing, ordering, shipping, returns, payment, and warranty.

Shipping

MARIS ships across Pakistan. Delivery charges and timelines are confirmed directly on WhatsApp after order review.

Returns

Unused footwear in original packaging can be reviewed for exchange or return support. Contact MARIS on WhatsApp.

Sizes

Most styles fit true to size. If between sizes, share your usual sneaker size through WhatsApp for guidance.

Payment

MARIS currently uses direct WhatsApp checkout instead of online payment. Payment method is confirmed during order finalization.

Delivery

Delivery address, contact number, and product availability are verified before dispatch.

Warranty

Manufacturing concerns are reviewed case-by-case. Damage from misuse or heavy wear is not covered.
