Privacy Policy

Privacy, handled with restraint.

This sample policy explains how MARIS may handle customer details for orders, support, and newsletters.

Information we collect

MARIS may collect your name, phone number, email, city, delivery address, selected product, size, color, and quantity when you contact us or place an order through WhatsApp.

How we use information

Information is used to confirm orders, arrange delivery, answer support questions, manage exchanges, and send newsletter updates if you subscribe.

WhatsApp ordering

When you click a WhatsApp button, your device opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled order message. WhatsApp’s own policies also apply.

Data protection

MARIS should only keep customer information as long as needed for business, support, and legal purposes.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact hello@maris.pk.
